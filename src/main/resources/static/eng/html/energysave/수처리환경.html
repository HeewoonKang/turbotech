Wastewater Treatment

1. STRONGLY DIFFERENTIATED PROCESS ENVIRONMENT STABILITY

The pressurized air is distributed throughout the tank to create a pressure greater than the pressure at the back due to the height of the water. In particular, when air bubbles are generated at the bottom of the aeration tank through the aeration pipe, the pressure air is dispersed. In order to satisfy these requirements, the turbo blower and turbo compressor must be able to control the flow rate within a certain condition range of the upper space of the tank while continuously generating pressure. Turbowin can handle overwhelmingly greater flow than older/lower-end blowers and compressors with turndown ratios in the 40-100% range. In addition, Turbowin's Anti-Explosive certification ensures the safety of workers in wastewater treatment plants where explosive gases may be scattered.

2. OVERWHELMING ENERGY SAVING EFFICIENCY AND BOOSTER EFFECT

Overwhelming energy saving efficiency and booster effect In order to chemically decompose or destroy organic matter, or to supply oxygen to bacteria, the boosting effect of Turbowin's turbo blowers and turbo compressors is essential for continuous batch reactor treatment, membrane bioreactor treatment, activated sludge treatment and many processes in industrial, municipal sewage or wastewater treatment plants. In addition, up to 70% or more of the energy consumed by wastewater treatment plants is used for aeration, and the energy required by the unit costs 5 to 10 times more than the cost of blower and compressor equipment. Therefore, the key to success in the wastewater treatment industry is to keep your total operating costs low, leading to lower energy costs. Turbowin products boasting the world's highest energy-saving efficiency provide users with a very good ROI.

3. LOW NOISE LEVEL, CONVENIENT MAINTENANCE AND USER CONVENIENCE

Turbowin Turbo blower and Turbo compressor are state-of-the-art compressed air solutions with very low noise levels, eliminating the need for additional investment in separate noise reduction devices. In addition, compared to older/low-end blowers and compressors, it provides strong product durability guaranteed by the state-of-the-art turbo technology armed with patents such as dual cooling system and global technology certification, as well as convenient maintenance by periodically replacing only the medium air filter even while the machine is in operation. It is a remote control system that utilizes maintenance and IoT functions to provide extreme user convenience.
