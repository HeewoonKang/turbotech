Bio Chemical

1. EXTREME COMPRESSED AIR PURITY

The biochemical & pharmaceutical industry processes in nano units, and among the Big 5 process elements, water, electricity, gas, heat, and pressure air, compressed air is demanding the world's highest level of stringent standards. All require extreme purity and no contaminants are allowed in the air to ensure the highest quality production. Turbowin's turbo blower and turbo compressor are total solutions that provide 100% pure pressure air. When the world's best energy saving efficiency is combined with this extreme purity of compressed air, the three goals of optimal production cost reduction, quality achievement, and user convenience can all be achieved.

2. A WORLD-CLASS, COMPLETE CLEAN AIR SOLUTION

In the biochemical & pharmaceutical manufacturing process, cleanliness is very important. All contaminants such as dust and moisture must be removed, and the room temperature must be controlled. Turbowin's turbo blower and turbocompressor smartly and powerfully provide the perfect clean air required in the biochemical & pharmaceutical industry in the Industry 4.0 era. This capability of Turbowin is proving its superiority by introducing Turbowin's turbo products to global leading companies that currently produce bio, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and fertilizers.
