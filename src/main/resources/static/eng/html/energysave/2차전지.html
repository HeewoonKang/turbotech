Secondary Battery

Secondary Battery / Hydrogen Fuel Cell

Since 2018, TurboWin has been meticulously preparing turbo compressors for secondary battery applications. Electric or hybrid vehicles require a large number of lithium-ion battery cells, which are typically assembled into battery packs that can be integrated into electric vehicles. Each battery cell includes several components such as anodes, cathodes, and electrolytes. TurboWin's turbo compressors are designed to support various secondary battery manufacturing processes, from material preparation for battery cells to the supply of compressed air used in the processes. TurboWin employs specialized engineers who are well-versed in the electric vehicle battery manufacturing process to optimize the most sustainable combination of equipment. They combine air and gas compressors with the right dryers, filters, and gas generators to continuously supply clean, dry air at the lowest lifecycle cost.

Internal Combustion Engine Vehicles and Parts

TurboWin's key engineers come from automotive motor research institutes and have a deep understanding of automotive and parts manufacturing plants. From paintwork to assembly and pressing operations, our turbo products are suitable for all application areas within the automotive and parts manufacturing processes, helping to run factories efficiently and effectively with minimal cost and maximum impact. Compressed air is used for transporting raw materials, plastics, and more.

Painting & Coating

Laminating and testing of windshields / Assembly

Compressed air is used in processes such as cutting, transportation, lamination treatment, and testing of windshields in automotive and parts manufacturing plants.
