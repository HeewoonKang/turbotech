Semiconductor

1. A WORLD-CLASS, COMPLETE CLEAN AIR SOLUTION

In the semiconductor electronics manufacturing process, cleanliness is very important. All contaminants such as dust and moisture must be removed and the room temperature controlled. Turbowin's turbo blower and turbo compressor smart and powerfully provide the perfect clean air required in the semiconductor electronics industry in the Industry 4.0 era. This capability of Turbowin is proving its superiority as Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix, as well as leading electronic semiconductor display companies in Korea and around the world, are all rushing to introduce Turbowin's turbo products.

2. EXTREMELY HIGH PURITY OF COMPRESSED AIR

The semiconductor industry is demanding world-class, very stringent standards for compressed air, an important factor in processes such as sawing and cleaning computer chips and then packing them onto chips/boards and cooling the chips through compressed air and vacuum systems. . All require extreme purity and no contaminants are allowed in the air to ensure the highest quality production. Turbowin's turbo blower and turbo compressor are total solutions that provide 100% pure pressure air. When the world's best energy saving efficiency is combined with this extreme purity of compressed air, the three goals of optimal production cost reduction, quality achievement, and user convenience can all be achieved.
