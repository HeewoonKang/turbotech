Shipbuilding & Marine

As the International Maritime Organization's regulatory plan that requires all ships around the world to use marine fuel with a sulfur oxide (SOx) content of less than 0.5% comes into effect from 2020, major companies in the global shipbuilding and offshore industry need eco-friendly ship technology. Turbowin's turbo blower and turbo compressor products are based on the world's best turbo technology, and even in extreme marine environments, energy saving efficiency, cost savings, and stability far exceed the international standards of the International Maritime Organization. In the shipbuilding and maritime industry, in accordance with the strengthened International Maritime Organization (IMO) regulations, it is environmentally friendly to complex systems such as engines and generators, desulfurization and exhaust gas purification devices, electrical and automation systems, gas solutions, thrusters and gears, and seals and bearings. It is a very complex and demanding challenge that requires both high efficiency and high convenience and stability. Turbowin is the world's best eco-friendly turbo equipment company for shipbuilding and offshore industry, equipped with international certifications for moisture-proof, waterproof, and flame-retardant, as well as unrivaled international technology certification that shines in US classification certification. In particular, Turbowin, where international shipbuilding engineering companies and global marine leading companies are eagerly seeking cooperation, has prepared the essence of eco-friendly, high-value-added ship technology with pride and responsibility as a company with the world's best turbo technology. Through this, the turbo has a record-breaking reduction in weight and volume per unit, while the number of installed units is significantly reduced while possessing superior marine stability than inland, with tremendous energy saving efficiency, significant maintenance effort and cost reduction, and user convenience. Turbowin's technology is the source of competitiveness that leads the global shipbuilding and offshore industry in the 21st century.
