WL - t

WL-t Series is the world's first turbo blower with three cores and six impellers, covering up to 2,100 HP in a single turbo blower, with extreme energy efficiency, durability, and user convenience. WL-t Series eliminates the burden of installing multiple old-fashioned blowers and controls all together with a single WL-t Series, making it very efficient and convenient to manage with cost savings.
