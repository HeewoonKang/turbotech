UHEPM Motor

The UHEPM Motor is based on Turbowin’s global patents and certification technology and internal structural unique design. The motor’s energy-saving performance and durability exceeds the IE4 class.

It is a non-contact, permanent magnet-based motor with a pressure range from 420 Torr to 9.5 Bar and flow range from 3 HP to 2,100 HP based on the rotor rotation from 20,000 RPM to 320,000 RPM.
