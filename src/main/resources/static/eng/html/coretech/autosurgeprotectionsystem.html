Auto Surge Protection System

Surge has been called as the “Covid virus of pneumatics” and has always been a very powerful obstacle, especially for compressors and blowers.

Turbowin has designed the globally patented sub-solenoid valve to operate at least 3-4 times to cushion the impact and provide effective automatic control to prevent the turbo compressors and the turbo blowers from breaking, even if the operating point enters the surge zone due to unexpected factors during product operation.

This prevents sudden shutdown, core breakage and explosions, keeping the machinery, field-site and users safe.
